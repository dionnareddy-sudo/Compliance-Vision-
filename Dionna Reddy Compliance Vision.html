⚠ High Priority — 42 CFR Part 2 Compliance (Addiction Services)

The Consortium's Addiction Services and MAT programs are subject to 42 CFR Part 2 — federal law governing confidentiality of substance use disorder records. This requires dedicated policies, explicit patient consent for all disclosures, staff training, and audit protocols separate from HIPAA. Establishing a standalone 42 CFR Part 2 compliance framework will be a Day 1 priority.
